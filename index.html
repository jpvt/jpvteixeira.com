About me

Hi! I'm currently a last year Computer Engineering undergraduate at UFPB (class of 2023.1) doing research in Machine Learning and Medical Imaging. I'm a member of the Artificial Intelligence Applications Laboratory, UFPB since April 2019. I'm currently working in a research project in partnership with the X-ray Physics Lab, UPenn.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I co-founded TAIL (Technology and Artificial Intelligence League), UFPB. Our student-led research group is committed to spreading knowledge about artificial intelligence in our community. We aim to build a strong network of AI developers and foster collaboration among students from different backgrounds.

Outside the lab, I've gained valuable experience through internships at Amazon, and Rei do Pitaco, where I honed my skills in software development. I'm also a proud recipient of the Fundação Estudar Tech Fellow Program (One of 0.6% approved over 4285 applications in 2022), which has further inspired me to pursue a career in research and make a meaningful impact in the world.

In my spare time I like swimming, playing RPGs, and reading.
